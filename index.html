Moran Beladev

I’m an Information Systems Engineering M.Sc graduate from Ben-Gurion University under Lior Rokach and Bracha Shapira supervision. Now doing my Ph.D studies, researching NLP aspects in temporal graphs.

I have 5 years of experience in data science, deep learning, data modeling, algorithms and pipeline design.

Currently a Data Science Team Leader at Diagnostic Robotics, building ML solutions for the medical domain and NLP algorithms to extract clinical entities from medical visit summaries.
